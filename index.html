The Real Problem

Most dispensaries don't have an effort problem.
They have a systems problem.

Your staff is showing up. Your managers are putting in the work. You already have SOPs — maybe a whole binder of them.

But the problems keep coming back. The same mistakes, the same inconsistencies, the same conversations you've had ten times before.

That's not a people problem. That's an operational gap. The distance between having a process on paper and actually running that process consistently across every staff member, every shift, every day.

That gap is where most dispensaries lose ground — and most consulting advice doesn't touch it.

  • Staff completing the same task differently depending on who's working
  • Managers handling situations inconsistently or not enforcing standards
  • SOPs that exist but nobody actually follows in practice
  • The same operational issues coming up week after week
  • Owners pulled back into daily problem-solving instead of running the business
